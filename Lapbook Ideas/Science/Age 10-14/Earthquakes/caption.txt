Earthquakes

Eric, age 13

This lap book was a homework assignment after a 1 week review study.  Sources included National
Geographic Magazine, Volcanoes Earthquakes Ice thematic book by Dinah Zike (no longer available), 
ATI Wisdom Book, and Bob Jones Science.

Photo 2 contains Plates of Earth and pictures of damage.  Photo 2 shows folding and faulting, while
Photo 4 shows tsunami and earthquake extensions. Photo 5 shows a map of California earthquakes, and
6 shows the birth of an earthquake extension.  Number 7 contains a rock movement extension.
