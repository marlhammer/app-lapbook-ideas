Horses

Sarah, age 16

This lap book took a year to complete with mild parental involvement.  The sources for this were
numerous books, magazines, internet articles, and an interview with a veterinarian.

Photo 2 shows small booklets containing information about farrier tools, gauchos (spanish 
cowboys), barn fires, fly control, hyperthermia prevention, and feeding requirements.  Photo 3
shows booklets on bedding and a glossary.  4 shows booklets about colic, and another glossary while
5 shows pictures of booklets dealing with dental problems and rhinopneumonits (a horse disease).
